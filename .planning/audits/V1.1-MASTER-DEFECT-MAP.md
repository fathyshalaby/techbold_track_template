## 1) Executive summary of what is broken
This skeleton is partially connected but not cleanly composed for production-ready iteration. The largest risks are a broken event contract between backend and frontend, a duplicated/disconnected frontend surface, and toolchain baseline drift (package manager, scripts, and container parity). The result is a working skeleton with hidden disconnects and maintenance friction, not catastrophic source-level corruption.
